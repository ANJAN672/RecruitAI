# RecruitAI

**AI-Powered Recruitment Intelligence Platform**

> RecruitAI automates the entire recruitment pipeline — from understanding job descriptions to submitting scored candidates — reducing time-to-submission by 80%.

---

## The Problem

The $500B+ global recruitment industry still runs on manual processes and gut feel.

| Pain Point | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| **JD comprehension takes 30-60 min per role** | Recruiters manually read, extract skills, and interpret requirements before sourcing even begins |
| **Boolean search crafting is a bottleneck** | Building platform-specific queries for LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Dice requires specialized knowledge most recruiters lack |
| **Profile screening is subjective and inconsistent** | Manual resume-vs-JD comparison leads to missed candidates, wasted interviews, and bias |
| **Late submissions lose deals** | Slow workflows mean profiles reach clients after competitors have already moved |
| **No structured interview evaluation** | Feedback varies by interviewer, lacks standardization, and creates zero audit trail |
| **Salary benchmarking is guesswork** | Recruiters set ranges based on intuition, not data — misaligning client and candidate expectations |
| **Behavioral signals are ignored** | Cultural fit and soft-skill indicators buried in profiles go completely unscreened |
| **Pipeline tracking lives in spreadsheets** | No real-time visibility into submission status across jobs and clients |

**Result:** Staffing firms lose revenue to slow turnaround, mis-hires, and operational inefficiency.

## Product

### Core Capabilities

**1. AI JD Parsing**
Paste any job description. AI extracts role title, experience level, hard skills, soft skills, and certifications in under 5 seconds using pattern recognition and market intelligence context.

**2. Multi-Platform Boolean Search Generation**
One click generates 12 search queries — platform-specific booleans for LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Dice, CareerBuilder, and Monster, plus Google X-ray searches for all 6. Server-side enforcement (max 2 AND groups, 3 OR terms) ensures queries work on every platform.

**3. AI Candidate Scoring (0-100)**
Paste a candidate profile or resume. AI scores match quality (0-100), extracts skills, summarizes experience, and provides transparent match reasoning. Top 10 matches are highlighted with rank badges.

**4. Bulk Profile Import**
Import up to 20 candidate profiles in a single batch. AI scores each against JD requirements with real-time progress tracking. Results auto-sorted by match score.

**5. AI Behavioral Analysis**
Every ingested candidate receives an automated behavioral summary — communication style, leadership indicators, collaboration signals, career trajectory, red flags, and cultural fit assessment.

**6. Interview Intelligence**
- **Knowledge Guide:** AI generates plain-English explanations of technical concepts so non-technical HR recruiters can evaluate candidates independently
- **Interview Reports:** Paste raw interview notes → AI produces structured evaluation with strengths, weaknesses, and recommendation

**7. Market Intelligence**
Salary benchmarks (India LPA + US annual), market demand trends, and vetted training institutions (IITs, MIT, Stanford, IEEE, ACM — no EdTech platforms).

**8. Submission Tracker**
End-to-end pipeline: Sourced → Screened → Submitted → Interview → Offered → Joined. Visual status tracking, one-click updates, complete audit trail with timestamps.

**9. Excel Export**
Download candidate data as .xlsx — rank, name, match score, skills, experience, reasoning, status, LinkedIn URL. Ready for client submission with zero manual formatting.

**10. Role-Based Dashboards**
- **Recruiter Dashboard:** Full access to all AI features, sourcing, tracking, and export
- **Candidate Portal:** Self-service view of applications, match scores, and pipeline progress
